
DSC Club in our college is looking for a core team member who has basic knowledge of competitive programming. For that existing team created a contest and the ranks were released after the contest.
You're given ranks of NN people with their namesnames. your   task is to select only N/2 people based on their ranks   and display their names in the ascending order of their ranks 
In case if N is odd consider the floor(rounds downs the nearest integer) of N/2
The rank of N students can be anything may/may  not be  in the range 
1≤R(i)≤N
Input:

The first line contains the value of N.
Second contain N ranks. 
3rd line contain respective  names. 

Output:

Print the names of selected  people  each in a single line.

Constraints:

1≤N≤200
1≤N(i)≤1000

Sample Input:
4
1 3 4 2
Abhilash
akhil
vineeth
vijay

Sample Output:
Abhilash
vijay

EXPLANATION:
Here among 4 students ranks 1 and 2 were leading.Hence they were selected and printed based on  order  of their ranks .
