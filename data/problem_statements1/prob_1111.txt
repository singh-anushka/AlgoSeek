your words dance in the limelight, 
people wait for your first sight.

Input Format

First line will contain T, number of testcases. Then the testcases follow.
Each testcase contains of a single line of input, one integer N.

Output Format
For each testcase, output either "yes" or "no".
Constraints

1≤T≤1000
0≤N≤109

1634

467



                        Sample Output 1
