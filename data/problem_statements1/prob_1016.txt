
          
            Chef has A balls in his bag and he wants to distribute it among B People.
Chef wants to make sure, everyone gets equal amount of balls.
Chef is unable to make this calculation and needs your help.

Input:

1. The first line will input T test cases.
2. The following T lines will contain A , B as space separated inputs 
Output:
1. Print a single Integer - the number of balls each gets.
If balls cannot be distributed, print "cannot distribute" (without quotes)


Constraints:


 1 <= T <= 1000
 0 <= A , B <= 1e18
Example Input:

2
10 3
12 14

Example Output:

3
cannot distribute

Explanation:

Test Case 1: 10 balls, 3 people - everyone gets 3 balls.
