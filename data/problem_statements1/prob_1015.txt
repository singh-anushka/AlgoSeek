
While playing in the plains, Chef found a point AA with coordinates (X,Y)(X,Y). 
Chef is interested in finding the number of straight lines passing through the point AA such that their intercepts on both axes are positive integers. 
Input Format

First and only line of input contains two integers X and Y - the coordinates of the point A.

Output Format

For each test case, print an integer - the number of lines satisfying the given conditions.

Constraints

1≤X≤1012
1≤Y≤1012

3 2



                        Sample Output 1
                        





4
