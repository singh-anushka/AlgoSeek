
Vasya likes too much of Chocolates . Once she was watching “Thomas the Train” Cartoon, she dreamed how good it would be if trains were made up of chocolates , and not only trains but the rivers too.
She shares this dream with his younger sister . Suddenly her mother calls her for some work.
Now her younger sister “Miami” is thinking to cut a piece of Chocolate from that imaginary chocolate river.
But she didn’t like any obstacles in her piece of chocolate, such as an in-between tree, small mud island, or any other thing which are represented as 1 and wants a clean square piece of chocolate.
The rest of the river is represented as 0.
Each piece/unit of the river can be expressed as a cell of a matrix. She wants the maximum possible Sized Chocolate. But she is just a kid 3 years old and cannot figure out the size of the chocolate.
Help her find the maximum possible size of the chocolate.
Input:

Two Integers N, M.
Next N lines follows, 
each line having M integers.
Matrix can have 0 or 1 possible Values.
1 : Obstacle
0 : River with Chocolate

Output:
Print the maximum possible size of the Chocolate.
Constraints 
0 <= N,M <= 10^3.
Sample Input:

    3 3
    1 1 0
    1 1 1 
    1 1 1

Sample Output:
1
